Three Critical Challenges

Systemic Issues Preventing Fair Market Access

Intermediaries capture 30-40% of crop value through price manipulation. When market rate is ‚Çπ2,000/quintal, farmers receive only ‚Çπ1,400, losing ‚Çπ600 per transaction.

Forced Distress Sales

Lack of storage infrastructure forces immediate post-harvest sales when prices are lowest. Farmers cannot wait for optimal market conditions, losing 15-20% potential income.
