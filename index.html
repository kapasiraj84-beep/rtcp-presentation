üîç Core Issues Facing Farmers

Problem 1: Middlemen Exploitation
Middlemen control the supply chain and take 30-40% of farmer's income. When market price is ‚Çπ2,000 per bag, farmers only receive ‚Çπ1,200-1,400. The middleman keeps ‚Çπ600-800 without adding real value.
Problem 2: Information Asymmetry
Farmers have no access to real-time market prices. They depend on middlemen who can manipulate prices. No transparency in pricing leads to exploitation.
Problem 3: Forced Immediate Sale
Lack of storage facilities forces farmers to sell immediately after harvest when prices are at their lowest. Unable to wait for better market conditions.
